Add hover-to-peek to the collapsed left navbar. When the sidebar is collapsed, hovering the icon rail (after a short delay that ignores accidental passes) expands it as an overlay above the page content without reflowing the layout. Submenus are clickable while peeking, the logged-in user section expands, and the rail collapses again when the cursor leaves. Keyboard focus also opens the peek.
